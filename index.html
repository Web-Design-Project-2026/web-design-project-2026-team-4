About Tessan Studio

We capture your pet’s personality in a natural and timeless way. How it works

Here are the steps to help you undersatnd how our process works:

Step One

Getting Started

Write to us using the contact form on our page. Please tell us a bit about your ideas for the photoshoot, if you have any location in mind and preferred dates. We will get back to you in three days.

Step Two

Planning

Now that we know you would like to have your pet's photos taken, we can settle on the date and location. If you would like to have an outdoor photoshoot but don't exactly know where, we can recommend you locations based on our previous shoots. If you're more comfortable at your own home, we can arrange that as well.

Step Three

Photoshoot

We arrive to the location and the photoshoot begans. We start by taking a few minutes to get to know your dog and make them comfortable in front of the camera. You can bring their favorite toys and snacks, but we will have some things with us as well to get your dog's attention. Don't worry about the leash being on your dog, it will be Photoshopped out later. When the session ends, we will take a look at the photos and select them together. We can also arrange a separate appointment for this if it's more convenient for you.

Step Four

Enjoy

Usually after two weeks of selecting the photos, we will send the final ones to you online. Then you can access the gallery for a month, so make sure to download all photos before that. If you would like, you can print them, frame them, post them on social media, but most importantly, enjoy them for years to come.
