Our Photographers

Tessan Lindstöm

Tessan grew up in rural Sweden, surrounded with animals: her parents had a farm with horses, cows, sheep and dogs. Her love for photography started in high school when she worked as the photographer for the school paper. She went to study journalism in London, but photography always stayed part of her life, at this point just as a hobby. For a few years she worked as a journalist in London, but later moved home to Sweden and started to photograph Swedish landscapes. She later published these photos in a book and slowly trasitioned from landscapes to animals.

She has an eye for capturing special moments, and her calm presence can settle into the moment even the most energetic pets, resulting in photos that feel alive and authentic. Her style in warm, natural, and full of life.

Erik Sundqvist

Erik's journey into photography began with him taking pictures of his sisters playing dress-up. He later studied photography and began working in editorial. After spending years in studios, he decided to team up with Tessan and cresate their own studio.

Now working as a pet photographer, he brings years of experience to every stuido photoshoot. He enjoys working with both pets and their owners, capturing little interactions and shared moments. His relaxed and friendly approach helps the sessions feel effortless and fun, giving natural results full of character that tell a story beyond the image itself.

Linnea Dahlberg

Linnea originally studied portrait photography in Gothenburg, where she became fascinated by the subtle expressions that reveal personality. While she started with human portraits, she soon discovered a special interest in animals and the challenge of capturing their individuality in a controlled setting. This led her to focus on studio-based pet photography.

In her work today, Linnea combines technical precision with a gentle, reassuring presence. She creates a quiet, comfortable studio environment where pets feel safe and unpressured. Her style is clean and timeless, with carefully shaped light that highlights every detail, from the sparkle in a dog’s eyes to the texture of a cat’s fur.
